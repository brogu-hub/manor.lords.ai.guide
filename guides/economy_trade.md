# Economy and Trade Guide

## When to Build a Trading Post

- After basic food and housing are stable (usually mid-Year 1 or Year 2)
- When you have excess of one resource and shortage of another
- When your region lacks a key resource (no stone deposits, no iron)

## What to Export

- **Excess food** — berries and meat if you have surplus
- **Planks** — high value, easy to produce with Sawpit
- **Ale** — very high trade value if you have the production chain
- **Leather/Linen** — if you have surplus clothing materials

## What to Import

- **Stone** — if your region has no quarry
- **Iron** — for tools and weapons
- **Food** — emergency imports during winter shortages

## Regional Wealth

- Regional Wealth determines what goods traders bring
- Higher wealth = better goods available
- Wealth increases from successful trades and surplus production
- Keep regional wealth positive — bankruptcy reduces trade options

## Trade Tips

- Traders visit periodically — you can't control when they arrive
- Multiple Trading Posts don't speed up trader visits
- Trade routes can be set up for automatic recurring trades
- Always keep some gold reserve (Lord's Personal Wealth) for emergency purchases
