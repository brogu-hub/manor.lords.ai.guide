# Winter Survival Guide

## Resource Targets Per Family

- **Food:** 15-20 units per family minimum to survive winter
- **Firewood:** 3-4 units per family per winter month (approx 12-16 per family for full winter)
- **Clothing:** Each family needs at least 1 clothing item to avoid approval penalty

## Seasonal Timeline

### Autumn (Preparation Phase)
- Check firewood stockpile — target 15+ per family before winter
- Ensure food stockpile covers 4 months of consumption
- Stop non-essential construction that uses workers who should be gathering resources
- Farming fields should be harvested before frost

### Winter (Survival Phase)
- No farming possible — fields are frozen
- Berry gathering slows significantly
- Hunting remains viable but yields less
- Construction slows in heavy snow
- Firewood consumption increases

### Spring (Recovery Phase)
- Resume farming as soon as ground thaws
- Replant fields immediately
- Check if any families left during winter (approval drops)
- Rebuild food stockpiles for next year

## Emergency Actions

If food is critically low in winter:
1. Assign all available workers to Hunting Camps
2. Check if Trading Post can import food
3. Reduce consumption by pausing ale distribution
4. If families are starving, this is the #1 priority above everything else

If firewood runs out:
1. Assign workers to Woodcutter's Lodge immediately
2. If no timber, assign loggers to Logging Camp first
3. Consider buying firewood from traders
