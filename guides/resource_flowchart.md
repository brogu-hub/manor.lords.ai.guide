# Manor Lords Resource Flowchart (v0.8.050+)

Complete production chains and building relationships.

## Food Production
- **Farmhouse** → Wheat, Rye, Barley, Flax (raw crops)
- **Windmill** → Wheat flour, Rye flour (from grain)
- **Bakery** → Wheat bread, Rye bread (from flour, 4x bread per flour)
- **Communal oven** → Bread (simpler, from flour)
- **Vegetable garden** → Carrots, Beetroots, Cabbages
- **Forager hut** → Berries, Herbs, Mushrooms
- **Fisherman's hut** → Carp, Eel
- **Orchard** → Apples, Quinces, Pears
- **Chicken coop** → Eggs, Chickens
- **Apiary** → Wax, Honey
- **Hunter's camp** → Pelts, Small game (meat)
- **Animal pen** → Goats, Pigs → Milk, Chevon, Pork
- **Butcher's workshop** → Sausages, Mutton, Hides (from sheep/livestock)
- **Livestock trading post** → Sheep, Lambs

## Fuel & Timber
- **Logging camp** → Timber (raw logs from forest)
- **Forester's hut** → Plants new trees (no output)
- **Woodcutter's lodge** → Firewood (1 timber → 1 firewood)
- **Sawpit** → Planks (1 timber → 5 planks)
- **Charcoal kiln** → Charcoal (2 timber → charcoal)

## Construction Materials
- **Stone gatherer camp** → Rubble stone (from surface stones)
- **Quarry** → Rubble stone (from rock deposits)
- **Stonemason's station** → Dressed stone (from rubble stone, 1 rubble → 1 dressed)
- **Lime kiln** → Mortar (from rough stone, 2 stone → mortar)
- **Mining pit** → Iron ore, Clay, Salt
- **Clay furnace** → Rooftiles (from clay, 1 clay → 1 rooftile)

## Clothing
- **Sheep farm** → Wool (from sheep)
- **Weaver's workshop** → Linen (from flax), Yarn (from wool)
- **Tailor's workshop** → Gambesons (2x leather), Clothes/Linen (1x linen), Clothes/Woolen (1x yarn)
- **Tannery** → Leather (from hides, 1 hide → 1 leather)
- **Cobbler's workshop** → Shoes (from leather)

## Industry & Crafting
- **Bloomery** → Iron slab (from iron ore, 1 ore → 1 slab)
- **Smithy** → Tools (from iron slab, 1 slab → tools)
- **Blacksmith's workshop** → Tools, Iron Parts, Spears, Polearms, Sidearms
- **Joiner's workshop** → Wooden parts, Small shields, Large shields (from planks)
- **Bowyer's workshop** → Crossbows, Warbows (from planks + iron parts)
- **Armory** → Helmets, Mail armor (2x iron), Plate armor (3x iron + 6x iron)

## Brewery
- **Malthouse** → Malt (from barley)
- **Brewery** → Ale (from malt, 1 malt → 2 ale)

## Key Production Ratios
- 1 Timber → 1 Firewood (Woodcutter)
- 1 Timber → 5 Planks (Sawpit)
- 2 Timber → Charcoal (Kiln)
- 1 Flour → 4 Bread (Bakery)
- 1 Hide → 1 Leather (Tannery)
- 1 Iron Ore → 1 Iron Slab (Bloomery)
- 1 Rubble Stone → 1 Dressed Stone (Stonemason)
