# Build Order Guide

## Year 1 Start (First 10 Buildings)

1. **Logging Camp** (2 workers) — timber is needed for everything
2. **Storehouse** — central storage, place near future market
3. **Well** — water access for nearby Burgages
4. **5 Burgages** (plots) — housing for starting families. Place along road with backyard extensions
5. **Woodcutter's Lodge** (1 worker) — converts timber to firewood
6. **Granary** (1 worker) — food storage and distribution
7. **Hunting Camp** or **Forager Hut** (2 workers) — first food source depending on map
8. **Marketplace** — required for food/goods distribution. Build stalls for food variety
9. **Church** — approval boost from spiritual fulfilment, covers radius
10. **Sawpit** (1 worker) — converts timber to planks for upgrades

## Key Principles

- Always build housing (Burgages) before production — homeless families leave
- Food production before expansion — one bad winter kills settlements
- Logging Camp is always first — timber gates everything else
- Marketplace needs stalls assigned for each food type to get variety bonus
- Place Burgages with backyard space for vegetable gardens or chicken coops (free food/resources)

## Common Mistakes

- Building too many production buildings before having enough workers
- Forgetting firewood — families freeze in winter without it
- Not building a Church early — approval penalty without it
- Placing Burgages too far from Marketplace — workers waste time walking
