# Approval Guide

## What Affects Approval

### Positive Factors
- **Food variety** — multiple food types in Marketplace stalls (+approval per type)
- **Church coverage** — families within Church radius get spiritual fulfilment bonus
- **Ale availability** — tavern serving ale gives significant approval boost
- **Clothing** — families with clothing are happier
- **Housing** — no homelessness, upgraded Burgages
- **Tax reduction** — lower taxes increase approval

### Negative Factors
- **Starvation** — #1 approval killer, immediate and severe
- **No firewood in winter** — families freeze, approval crashes
- **Homelessness** — families without housing leave quickly
- **Missing clothing** — penalty when clothing need triggers (population dependent)
- **No Church** — spiritual need unmet
- **High taxes** — direct approval penalty
- **Overcrowding** — too many families per Burgage

## Approval Thresholds

- **75+** — New families arrive regularly, fast growth
- **50-75** — Slow growth, occasional new arrivals
- **30-50** — Population stagnant, risk of families leaving
- **Below 30** — Families actively leaving, settlement declining

## Burgage Upgrade Requirements

### Level 1 → Level 2
- Backyard extension built (garden, chickens, or goats)
- Clothing available
- Food variety (2+ types)
- Church within radius

### Level 2 → Level 3
- All Level 2 requirements met
- Ale available
- Full clothing set
- High approval sustained
